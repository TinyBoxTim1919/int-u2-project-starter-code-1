What Are Microtransactions?

Microtransactions are small in-game purchases, often used to unlock
characters, skins, powerups, or continue gameplay.

  • Cosmetic purchases
    (e.g, skins, emotes)
  • Loot Boxs
    (randomized rewards)
  • Pay-to-win items
    (better weapons.stats)

Why It's A Problem

Microtransactions are often designed to keep you spending.
Using techniques like:

  • Randomized rewards (like gambling)
  • FOMO (fear of missing out on limited time items)
  • Targeting young audiences with flashy rewards

Players may spend hundreds without realizing it - and some even get addicted
